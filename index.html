Welcome to SkillSim

SkillSim is a dynamic learning platform where people can learn technical skills in a virtual environment. Our goal is to offer a wide variety of skills which we can teach to people in a virtual environment for a more affordable price.

Simulation software

Because the software works in a virtual environment people don't just learn through visually seeing something but also from being able to actively interact with the different objects in place. This will make for a better learning environment.

Electricity

Trying electricity on your own can be very dangerous. We therefore believe it is way more convient to learn it virtually if you cannot go to a place to learn in real life. In that case, you cannot mess it up or make some dangerous mistakes.

Cooking

Even though cooking seems simple, preparing more advanced meals can be real tricky. You'll have to use dangerous tools, such as sharp knives. If you're not used to these tools, it's way safer to learn how to handle them with VR first.

Healthcare

In this workfield, there are lives depending on your skills. It's not that easy to practice the things you need to know on real people. VR will help you with learning medical skills in a safer environment where you can make mistakes.

Renovation

There isn't always a place to work and learn how to plumb, build and renovate. That's why we support this workfield in our VR program. Without actually working in a building, it will feel like you are. You'll learn as quick as in real life.

Leave your mark

SkillSim allows other developers to join in on the process of bringing education to VR. We allow everyone to create their own courses which people can learn from. These will be posted in the community section and can later even be adopted in our standard courses if the course meets all the standards.

14 Day free trial

Since people can be sceptical towards learning in virtual reality, we would like to first give our customers a taste of what they can expect from us. Therefore we offer a 14 day free trial to all our new customers.

Advantages

  • We monitor your learning to further improve the experience

Levels

We offer different levels so that everyone can learn at their own pace. Test yourself by taking our exams to get to the next level. All the way from beginner to expert.

Before you start a level, you have to take a short test so we can measure your skill level. If you don't think we got it right you can always try the test again. A test is not necessary if you are taking the class through an educational institution and this is the designated subject.

Working individual or through an institution

You can take courses all by yourself if you're just interested in learning particular skills, but we also support several educational institutions that can provide classes for their students using our VR courses. You can check out all the different services we offer on our webshop.
